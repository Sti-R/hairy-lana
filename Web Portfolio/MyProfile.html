About me
My story begins on the island of Oahu. In 2009, I graduated from University of Hawaii with a BA in East Asian Language Literature focusing on Japan. Back then, one of my dreams was to learn the Japanese language and expose myself to the full glory of Japanese culture. I was very fond of Japan and wanted first-hand experience working and living abroad. I felt that if I could speak Japanese, I could learn more about the culture and perhaps even learn about the economic conditions. I was always fascinated with the business, language, food and culture aspect of things. Shortly after graduating, I got offered a job teaching English in Japan.
